Tanglewood Books

Rare & Used Bookstore in Vancouver

Located at the corner of Broadway and Vine, next door to Platform 7 and The Heritage Hair Salon, Tanglewood Books specializes in top quality used, out-of-print, rare and publisher-remaindered books. Whether you are seeking a piece of classic literature or browsing for a bestseller, we have a large selection of books at great prices.

About

Since 1993, at Tanglewood Books, we have brought a passion for literature and the written word to Vancouver. Our current owner, Joel Schillinger, and staff are dedicated to honing their interests in order to create a great book shopping experience for you. Whether you are reserving books for a university literature class or searching for a how-to book, we have the knowledge to help you find what you are looking for. Stop by and browse our shelves for a great read today.

Open 7 Days a Week

Whether you are searching for a specific hot-off-the-press title or have an interest in a certain subject area, we invite you to browse our shelves 7 days a week. From mysteries and children’s literature to eastern philosophy, sports and humour, we stock a wide variety of genres. Our knowledgeable staff can help you find the exact title you are looking for. We offer excellent daily pricing.

Cash or Trade for Books

At Tanglewood Books, we will pay cash or accept your books for trade most days between 10:00 AM and 4:00 PM. If you wish to inquire about selling your books, please call us.
